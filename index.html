Our Story

Where Every Woman
Has the Power to Rise

She Can Foundation is an NGO registered under the Indian Society Registration Act of 1860, affiliated with NITI Aayog — dedicated to uplifting and empowering underprivileged women across India.

Our mission is to provide education, training, and resources to help women from marginalized communities overcome obstacles and achieve their full potential. We believe that every woman deserves the opportunity to thrive — regardless of her background.

Through our pan-India programs, we have distributed over 1,20,000 free sanitary pad kits, conducted awareness workshops, and provided dignity kits to girls who need them most.

What We Do

Our Programs &
Initiatives

🩸
Dignity Kits Program
We distribute free sanitary pads and hygiene essentials to girls across India, ensuring they can stay in school and live with dignity.
🎓
Scholarship & Education
Providing scholarships, books, and learning support to girls from underprivileged families so education is never out of reach.
🌱
Skill Development
Vocational training, digital literacy, and entrepreneurship workshops that open doors to economic independence for women.
🤝
Community Outreach
Door-to-door awareness campaigns and community events that create safe spaces for women to speak up and seek support.
💻
Internship Program
Mentoring 50,000+ interns into change-makers, giving young people the tools to lead with ambition and empathy.
🏥
Healthcare Access
Organising health camps, mental wellness sessions, and connecting women with medical support in underserved areas.
